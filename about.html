About Rafeek Smiles Dental

Affordable, high-quality dentistry for all ages. Our gentle dentists use the latest technology for everything from cleanings to implants. Painless dentistry in a welcoming environment. Schedule now! Professionalism is key; this is why our staff uphold the dental codes of ethics to offer you affordable, professional dental services. We apply industry best practices, cutting edge technology, our experience and knowledge in dental care to provide premium and affordable services to all. We believe dental and oral care is a vital part of your health. That’s why preventive care is at the core of our philosophy and it’s why every member gets a personalised prevention and treatment plan.
